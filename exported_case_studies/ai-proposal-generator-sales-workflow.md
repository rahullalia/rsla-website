            <h3>Phase 1: Structured Discovery Call Capture</h3>
            <p>
                Post-call, sales completes a focused Typeform (5-10 minutes) capturing:
            </p>
            <ul>
                <li>Prospect details (name, company, email)</li>
                <li>One-line business summary</li>
                <li>The bleeding-neck problem they described</li>
                <li>The solution we proposed</li>
                <li>Deliverables and timeline commitments</li>
                <li>Pricing tier</li>
            </ul>

            <h3>Phase 2: Claude Opus 4 Generates Content</h3>
            <p>
                Make.com triggers instantly, feeding form data to Claude Opus 4, which:
            </p>
            <ul>
                <li>Generates a compelling proposal title</li>
                <li>Writes persuasive problem and solution narratives</li>
                <li>Breaks deliverables into 5 crystal-clear scope items</li>
                <li>Constructs a 4-milestone timeline with concrete dates</li>
                <li>Outputs structured JSON ready for template injection</li>
            </ul>

            <h3>Phase 3: Automated Template Population</h3>
            <p>
                Structured data flows directly into a branded Google Docs template:
            </p>
            <ul>
                <li>Professional design with RSL/A identity</li>
                <li>Client-specific business context</li>
                <li>Custom problem/solution narrative</li>
                <li>Detailed 5-deliverable scope of work</li>
                <li>4-phase timeline with milestone gates</li>
                <li>Investment summary and clear next steps</li>
            </ul>
